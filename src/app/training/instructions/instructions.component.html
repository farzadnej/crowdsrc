Instructions

Thank you for participating in this research study. You will be asked to watch a series of online videos and rate them on quality and acceptability, and occasionally answer some other questions about your video-watching experience. Please answer all questions as accurately as possible.


Some of the questions refer to the term “technical quality”. For the purposes of this experiment, technical quality will refer to the continuity and completeness of video playback. Therefore, a video that plays from start to finish with no freezing or interruptions would have good technical quality. Interruptions to the video and video failures reduce the technical quality.


The videos will be split into sessions (groups). There will be 10 sessions of 7 videos, for a total of 70 videos.


During the videos, you will have the option to express your opinion about the video using an on-screen panel. You can answer these questions at any time during the video. You do not need to answer these questions, only answer if you feel it is necessary.
